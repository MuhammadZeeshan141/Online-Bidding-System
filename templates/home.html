Online Bidding

Online bidding is the future of personal property & real estate auctions; it allows prospective buyers to bid from the comfort of their own home, while still maintaining the competitive nature of a traditional auction. Online auctions mainly break down and remove the physical limitations of live auctions such as geography, presence, time, space, and a smaller target audience. Auctions have evolved from live auctions to online auctions with the help of technology.

How Bidding Works

Sealed-bid auction, the process is incredibly simple. Bidders submit their bid for the auction at the same time and no one knows what the other person has bid. The highest bid is the winning bid. Unlike the English auction, there is no bidding war with the price being driven up by competing bidders. A potential buyer makes a bid, which is the amount that the buyer is willing to pay for that item. Online bidding system connects sellers with buyers from potentially anywhere in the world.
